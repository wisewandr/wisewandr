💡 Safety Tips
📱Add at least 2 contacts — one local if possible, one back home. WhatsApp works internationally even when roaming.
⏰Set check-in reminders for every evening. If you miss one, your contacts can text you before escalating.
📍Share your trip timeline before you leave — then contacts know your general itinerary even without real-time location.
🔋Keep a portable charger. Your safety check-in only works if your phone is on. Dead battery = no check-in.
🌐Download offline maps before visiting remote areas. GPS works without data — you can still share a location pin.
